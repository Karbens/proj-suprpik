The CONTEST Administrator may add matchups at any time; however, the majority of matchups will be available on the contest page at 9:00 AM ET on the day of the event.

    Here are examples of matchups between two teams:
    • NCAA FOOTBALL - Oregon Ducks at UCLA Bruins
    • NFL - Denver Broncos at Kansas City Chiefs
    • Soccer - Arsenal at AC Milan
    • NHL - Buffalo Sabres at Philadelphia Flyers
    Here are examples of "prop" matchups between teams, players or a combination of both:
    • NFL - Tom Brady Pass Completions vs. New York Jets 1st Downs
    • MLB - Colorado Rockies Hits vs. Jake Peavy Strikeouts
    • GOLF - Brandt Snedeker Lower 1st Round vs. Ernie Els Lower 1st Round
    • NBA/MLB - Philadelphia 76ers points scored vs. Cole Hamels pitch count
    For most prop matchups the side with the HIGHER score, time or total will be declared the winner, but in some, the side that is LOWER will win. Please read the prop matchup questions and corresponding opponents carefully before making your pick.

    All participants start at zero. If you win a matchup your streak continues; if you lose it, you're back to 0. Your streak is measured by the number of consecutive outcomes you correctly selected from the officially listed matchups.

    You are not required to play every day, but you must make at least one selection every five days. Matchups that end as a tie will be scored as a "PUSH" and your streak will remain intact. Matchups may also be cancelled by the Game Administrator for reasons including (but not limited to) player injuries, player absences, or inability to complete the match because of weather or any other reason; if this occurs, the matchup will be emailed to customers listed as "No Action", Cancelled", "Postponed" or "OFF" and scored as "PUSH". In all such cases, the Game Administrator's decision is final, binding and non-appealable. In the event that the matchup you selected is marked as a "PUSH", your streak will NOT end, but rather your record remains at the same number prior to the "Push"

    Please note, you must make at least one selection every five days.
